A ROAD TRIP THROUGH SCOTLAND

A journey through
Lochs and Legends

From the pulsating city lights of Glasgow, the road follows the shores of Loch Lomond and cuts through the towering glens of Glen Coe before reaching the rugged landscapes of Skye and the Outer Hebrides. It then turns east through Inverness and the Cairngorms, passes ancient castles, coastal villages and wild rivers, and ends in Edinburgh during the city’s extraordinary Fringe season of theatre, song, dance, music and food.
