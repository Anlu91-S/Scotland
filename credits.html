Photography and data credits

Destination and trail photographs are either project-owned/generated assets already included in the roadbook or freely licensed images loaded from Wikimedia Commons. Individual Wikimedia file names are defined in app.js and resolve to their original file pages and licences through Wikimedia Commons.

Trail route links and community snapshots point to AllTrails. Maps and live place searches point to Google Maps. Restaurant, campsite and attraction details link to the relevant operators and destination organisations. No third-party review text or protected photo gallery has been copied into this project.
